Clubs and Organizations

Society of Young Computer Professionals

The Society of Young Computer Professionals (Sycomp) is a co-curricular organization comprising Information Technology and Computer Science students from Holy Name University. Its mission is to foster knowledge sharing, educational advancement in IT, and alignment with the rapidly evolving IT industry.

Google Developer Student Club of Holy Name University

The Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) of Holy Name University is a vibrant community that provides a platform for individuals, beyond tech enthusiasts, to showcase talents, connect, and grow as part of a unified community.
